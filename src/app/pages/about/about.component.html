With this platform, we believe everyone deserves an opportunity to learn and grow. We wanted to create a platform to help developers learn, teach, and make a profit from streaming. Our goal is to remove any technical or financial barriers that may prevent new or veteran programmers from growing their tech stack knowledge. We're excited to help you on your journey! We mean that. If we can help atleast 10 developers. we'd be pretty happy. :)

About Company


Code Crow Corp


was started by a developer in his room. He knew he needed to create a startup when his buddy in college suggested making a gaming company with their last names combined (Singing Lemon). He spent years working on mobile games in Unity 3D, that never launched. The company pivoted from mobile gaming, to crypto currency apps, and now web. All the while founders came and left. The idea for Code Crow came to fruition when he realized he needed to create a platform that allowed students to get help on their assignments in real time. He hired an amazing group of developers that built the foundation of the platform. Together, they launched Code Crow!
